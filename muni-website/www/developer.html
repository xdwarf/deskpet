The Maker

Proud Danish Dad.
Viking-Looking Maker.

I'm a dad, a maker, and a developer based in Denmark. I like building things that sit on your desk and make you feel something — a bit of presence, a bit of personality, a tiny creature that knows when you've been away.

Muni started as a weekend project. Then the firmware got interesting. Then I ordered a round display. Then I found myself designing Norse sprite sheets at midnight. That's how it always goes.

I share everything openly — firmware, schematics, Docker images, the lot. If you want to build one yourself, every file you need is on GitHub. If you want one that just works, I'll sort you out.
